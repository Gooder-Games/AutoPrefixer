## Why AutoPrefixer?

Standard naming conventions are critical for large-scale Unreal Engine projects. **AutoPrefixer** automates this tedious process, ensuring that every asset follows the [Epic Games Standard Naming Convention](https://docs.unrealengine.com/5.0/en-US/recommended-asset-naming-conventions-in-unreal-engine-projects/).

	</Card>
	<Card title="Smart Scrubbing" icon="add-document">

## Features at a Glance

- **Native C++ Performance**: Fast, reliable, and integrated directly into the editor context menu.
- **Support for 50+ Classes**: Pre-configured with defaults for almost every standard Unreal Engine asset type.
- **Error-Proof Logic**: Prevents double-prefixing and handles naming collisions gracefully.
- **Organization Support**: Automatically routes renamed assets into predefined subfolders.
- **Multi-Version Support**: Compatible with Unreal Engine 5.1, 5.2, 5.3, 5.4, and 5.5.
